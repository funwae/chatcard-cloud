# Abuse & Support

## Reporting Abuse

If you encounter abuse, violations of our Acceptable Use Policy, or security issues:

**Email:** abuse@chatcard.cloud

Please include:
- Description of the issue
- Relevant URLs or proof multihash
- Screenshots or evidence (if applicable)
- Your contact information

We review all reports within 48 hours and take appropriate action.

## Support Channels

### General Support
**Email:** hello@chatcard.cloud

For questions about:
- Account issues
- Feature requests
- Technical problems
- Billing (if applicable)

### Security Issues
**Email:** security@chatcard.cloud

For:
- Security vulnerabilities
- Data breaches
- Unauthorized access

**Please do not report security issues publicly.** We will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours and provide updates on resolution.

## Response Times

- **Abuse reports:** 48 hours
- **Security issues:** 24 hours (acknowledgment)
- **General support:** 3-5 business days

## What We Do

When you report abuse, we:
1. Review the report within 48 hours
2. Investigate the issue
3. Take appropriate action (warnings, rate limiting, account suspension)
4. Notify you of the outcome (if you provided contact info)

## What We Don't Do

- Share reporter information with the reported party
- Take action without investigation
- Restore accounts that were suspended for clear violations

## Appeals

If your account was suspended and you believe it was in error, contact support@chatcard.cloud with:
- Your handle or email
- Reason for appeal
- Any relevant context

Appeals are reviewed within 5 business days.
